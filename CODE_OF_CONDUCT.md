# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Pique Esconde should be developed with respect, clarity and care. Contributors and testers are expected to communicate constructively and avoid harassment, intimidation, discrimination or abuse.

## Project Safety

Because this app involves real-world play and location, discussions should prioritize player safety, privacy and responsible use.

Do not encourage:

- unsafe physical play;
- trespassing;
- harassment or stalking;
- sharing private room codes publicly without consent;
- exposing GPS coordinates or personal data.

## Enforcement

Concerns can be reported to:

```txt
suporte@eduardobertin.com.br
```

Reports will be reviewed with the goal of protecting contributors, testers and players.

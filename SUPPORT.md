## What to Include

- device and operating system;
- browser or app build, if known;
- approximate time of the problem;
- room code, only if needed for support;
- what you expected to happen;
- what actually happened.

Do not send passwords, private keys, real GPS coordinates or documents unless specifically requested.

## Safety

If someone is using the app to harass, monitor, follow or intimidate people, stop playing, leave the room and contact support.
